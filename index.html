HOME REMODELING SERVICES

We know you have options when it comes to finding the right contractor to complete your home remodeling projects. At PRECISION HOME INNOVATION, we work with each client to answer questions and concerns to get the details done and make your remodeling experience a pleasant one.

BATHROOM REMODELS & CUSTOM SHOWERS

Our bathroom remodeling experts will offer remodeling expertise, product knowledge, and exciting design ideas to guide you through the process with ease. Bathroom remodeling is a wonderful way to add value to your home and pleasure to your hectic life. Whether you have a precise image in mind or are starting from scratch, our designs will transform your existing bathroom into a luxurious, functional and beautiful retreat. We provide extensive experience in remodeling showers, converting tubs to walk-in showers.

KITCHEN REMODELS & CUSTOM KITCHENS

Kitchens are the heart of the home and often the main gathering place. An amazing kitchen starts with artistic vision and proper space planning. We specialize in kitchen remodeling, from creating kitchen enhancements to expanding your kitchen, redesigning and extending your existing kitchen to achieve the look of spacious new homes. Whether you enjoy entertaining or just want a space that is efficient, well-designed, and functional. We especially focus on the construction of new cabinets and the installation of new countertops made of precious materials such as MARBLE, GRANITE and QUARTZ, as well as the replacement of splashbacks, obtaining a magical final result.
